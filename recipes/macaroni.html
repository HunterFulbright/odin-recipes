Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

Macaroni and cheese is a classic dish that can work as either a side dish or an entree. This recipe calls for a tasty mix of cheddar and parmesan cheese. Adding different types of cheese or your favorite seasonings allows you to customize this recipe however you see fit. Any way you enjoy it, this homemade mac and cheese is sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Noodles
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheddar and Parmesan Cheese
  • Seasonings - Salt, Pepper, Paprika
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Salt a pot of water and boil the macaroni noodles until they are al dente. Drain and move noodles to a baking dish.
  2. Melt the butter, then whisk in the flour, then whisk in the milk.
  3. Bring the mix to a simmer and stir in the cheeses, salt and pepper. Continue simmering until it gets thick.
  4. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.
  5. To make the toppping, melt two tablespoons of butter, add the bread crumbs, and toast in a skillet until the crumbs are brown.
  6. Spread the topping over the mac and cheese and season with paprika.
  7. Bake in the oven at 350 degrees until the topping is golden brown.
