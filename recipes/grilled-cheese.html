Jalapeno Popper Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

This jalapeño popper grilled cheese is an awesome spin on a plain grilled cheese sandwich. Feel free to alter it to your liking. The ciabatta bread is not typical grilled cheese material but the crunch of the buttery grilled chewy bread goes perfectly with this jalapeño filling. You can also use sourdough bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 Ounces Softened Cream Cheese
  • 1 Tablespoon Sour Cream
  • Jalapeno Pepper Slices
  • 2 Ciabatta Sandwich Rolls
  • 4 Teaspoons Butter
  • Crushed Tortilla Chips
  • 1/2 Cup Shredded Colby-Monterey Jack Cheese

Steps

  1. Combine cream cheese, sour cream, and jalapenos in a bowl. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cut bread rolls to make two bread slices. Spread 1 teaspoon butter on each slice of bread.
  3. Place 1/2 of the cream cheese mixture, 1/2 of the crushed chips, and 1/2 of the shredded cheese on the non-buttered side of the bottom bun.
  4. Place the top half of the bun on the sandwich and place the sandwich on the hot skillet.
  5. Grill until lightly browned and flip over, continue grilling until cheese is melted and the second side is golden brown.
