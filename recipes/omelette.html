Ham and Cheese Omelette

Description

This ham and cheese omelette is quick, easy, and most importantly, delicious! You can enjoy omelettes with a filling of any breakfast foods you want, but my favorite is this mixture of ham and cheese. It's a great breakfast option or can be used as a quick and easy dinner. Serve with a side of bacon for the best results!

Ingredients

  • 2 Teaspoons Vegetable Oil
  • Chopped Deli Ham Slices
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Teaspoons Chopped Chives
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1/4 Cup Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil on medium heat in a skillet. Add ham and cook, stirring frequently, until browned.
  2. Beat eggs with 1 teaspoon chives, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour mixture into skillet and cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Gently lift the edges of the omelette and tilt skillet to cook the rest of the omelette.
  5. Sprinkle cheese on top and fold omelette in half. Flip it and cook it until egg has fully cooked.
  6. Sprinkle remaining chives on top.
